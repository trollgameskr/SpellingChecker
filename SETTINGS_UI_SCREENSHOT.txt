INSTRUCTIONS:
================================================================================

1. Right-click the SpellingChecker icon in the system tray
2. Click "Settings" from the context menu
3. Scroll down to the "Hotkeys:" section
4. Find the "Variable Name Suggestion Hotkey:" field (highlighted above)
5. Click in the text box and type your desired hotkey combination
6. Click "Save" button
7. Restart the application for changes to take effect
